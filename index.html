about us

Why choose us?

Zygote Technologies (Pty) Ltd is a South African Telecommunications company dedicated to providing guaranteed cost saving communication and IT solutions. Our team is committed to after sales service by priding ourselves in quick turnaround times and a customer-centric attitude to aid our clients in the best way possible. We create offers by first conducting a communications / IT audit which allows us to understand the current business solution. We then apply our expert technical knowledge to then design a cost saving solution around those requirements. Every solution is tailored to the client’s specific needs. Zygote Technologies is a compliant company that prides itself on the ability to continuously communicate with clients, giving customers peace of mind, while assuring on-time delivery. We achieve these results by utilizing our ever growing, dedicated, highly qualified and experienced technical team. Zygote Technologies (Pty) Ltd is a accredited dealer of Telecommunications experience. In addition to our technical ability, passion, industrial knowledge and work ethic, we have also built a network of support structures through our interactions with our business partners, ISP’s, clients and product support specialists within all the leading Telco’s in the industry to assist us with providing the best support and solutions to our customers. All of the above have shown us the desperate requirement in the market to provide SMMEs with reliable, adequate and affordable telecommunication/IT solutions, and we abundantly equipped to fill this gap.
